## 📐 CAD Collaboration (STEP Files)
For advanced users and engineers, I have included **STEP files** to allow for easy modification and integration. 

- **Full Assembly Control:** Use the `.step` files to verify fits in your own CAD environment.
- **Customization:** Feel free to modify the design to create your own custom mounts or specialized jigs.
- **High Compatibility:** Compatible with all major CAD software including Fusion 360, SolidWorks, CATIA, and Rhino.

> [!TIP]
> If you create a derivative work or an improved version based on these STEP files, I’d love to see it! Please feel free to share your results with the community.
